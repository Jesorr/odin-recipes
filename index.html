Banana Bread

Description

We eat a lot of bananas at our house. Every time we go to the grocery store (maybe twice a month), we buy about a dozen bananas. For some that may not sound like much, but there's only three of us, and one is a toddler. We eat a lot of bananas, but a vast majority end up getting frozen and eaten later. They typically turn into fruit smoothies, but every now and then, they turn into banana bread.

Now we generally eat pretty healthy. We tend to eat sweets in moderation if at all, but to be quite honest, all restraint and self discipline goes out the window when we have banana bread at home. It's sweet, but not too sweet. It's nice and soft, but can have just the right amount of crunch with a few nuts. Warm this bad boy up and spread a little butter and you have heaven on a plate. Anywho, here it is!

Ingredients

  • 2-3 medium, peeled, very ripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
